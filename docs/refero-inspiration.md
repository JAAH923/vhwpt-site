## Proposed One-Page Structure

1. Hero: Bruce/company brand immediately visible, strong real image, concise promise, primary CTA `Book Consultation`, secondary CTA `View Services`.
2. Trust strip: credentials, years of experience, specialties, location/service area.
3. Service cards: physical therapy lens, personal training, online training, health evaluations.
4. Bruce section: headshot, short practitioner story, credentials, philosophy.
5. How it works: evaluate, plan, train/treat, progress.
6. Results/testimonials: quote cards or outcome-focused proof.
7. FAQ: insurance, appointment expectations, online vs in-person, evaluation process.
8. Contact CTA: form plus phone/email/location details.

## Direction

Combine Acuity's one-page completeness, Hers' healthcare trust language, ClassPass' fitness energy, and Calm's clean contact flow.
